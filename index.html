Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 20, 2026

Welcome to E&C Courier (ECCO). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, protect, and share your information in compliance with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and applicable Canadian privacy laws.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the information necessary to provide our courier services effectively. This includes:

  • Personal Information: When you create an account or place an order, we collect your name, email address, and phone number.
  • Location Data (Drivers Only): We track the real-time location of our drivers to facilitate dispatch and process deliveries. We never track the location of our customers.
  • Device and Usage Information: Our application is powered by Google Firebase. Firebase automatically collects basic diagnostic data to ensure the app functions securely. This may include your IP address, device type, operating system, and crash reports.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information strictly for the following business purposes:

  • To process, fulfill, and track delivery orders.
  • To notify customers regarding the status of their deliveries.
  • To dispatch drivers efficiently based on location.
  • To maintain, troubleshoot, and improve the functionality of our app and website.

3. Information Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Your data is only shared with essential service providers necessary to operate our business:

  • Google Firebase: We use Firebase for database hosting, user authentication, and app analytics. Your data is stored securely on Google's servers and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Security and Retention

We implement commercially acceptable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, or theft. All data is routed through secure, encrypted connections provided by Firebase. We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.

5. Your Rights and Account Deletion

You have the right to access, update, or request the deletion of your personal information at any time.

To request the deletion of your account and all associated data: Please send an email to admin@eandccourier.ca with the subject line "Account Deletion Request." We will process your request promptly and confirm once your data has been permanently removed from our systems.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our business practices or legal requirements. Continued use of our app or services after modifications constitutes your consent to the updated policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us at:

Email: admin@eandccourier.ca
