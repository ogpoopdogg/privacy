Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 20, 2026

E&C Courier (ECCO) respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Data Collection

  • Personal: Name, email, and phone number.
  • Location: We track Driver location only for delivery processing. We do NOT track customers.
  • Technical: Google Firebase collects basic device info for app stability.

2. Use of Data

Data is used to complete deliveries, notify customers, and improve app performance. We share necessary data with Google Firebase for hosting and analytics.

3. Account Deletion

To delete your account and data, email admin@eandccourier.ca.
